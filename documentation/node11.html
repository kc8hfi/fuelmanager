Display and Edit fuel mileage data

Here is the All Data screen
This is all the fuel mileage information for the currently selected vehicle. You can go here to edit the date, miles, gallons, and cost. The MPG will be automatically recalculated. Click on ``Save Changes'' to save your changes back to the database.

The total number of records is displayed at the bottom
